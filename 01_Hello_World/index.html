TRS-80 Color Computer

My original computer was a TRS-80 Color Computer 1 in the early 1980's. My parents had previously bought the TRS-80 Model 1 based on the Z-80 processor. Due to the price, they would not let me use that computer. I remember going through the manual and doing my first for loop. I have recreated part of that experience here. Below is 3 columns, the first is the 3 different models of the Coco computer. The second is my first program. At the time I did not know the significance of 255, ie. 2 to power of 8 minus 1 because counting starts at zero. My first program did not have me start at zero, but 1. The 3rd is representation of the first program.
